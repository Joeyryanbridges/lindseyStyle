​Lindsey M. Bridges








As a hairdresser I strive to make every client feel as uniquely themselves as I possibly can . I have been doing hair for 6 years and have loved every second of it. Not only do I strive to give my clients the best overall service and experience, I learn from ever person that sits in my chair. To contiue to better not only my craft, but the entire service and overall experience!

Toni and Guy trained in Scottsdale Arizona, I spent the first few years of my career shadowing amazing mater cutting specialists. I worked with the actual Toni and Guy company for 2 years then decided it was time to move on. Wanting to enhance my color skills I moved to become an independent stylist and have been ever since.

I love all things hair and have experience in most every type of service so I do not specialize in any one thing inparticular. I contine to educate myself on a regular basis on all types of services. That being said I love balayage and mens barbering if I had to chose a favorite.
